Shiny Stock App

Luiz Bergo

02/03/2020

Shiny Presentation

Problems in controlling the flow of supplies IN and OUT from your Businness? This app may help you to wipe out the mess. Few clicks and control of your stock.

A use case in a Pizzeria is available here: Shiny Pizza Stock App.

Control the good you receive

Just update the amount receive for each good.

For example:

## [1] " Received  20  Units of supply A:"
## [1] " Received  50  Units of supply B:"
## [1] " Received  35  Units of supply C:"

Control the orders

Inform ordered itens and automatically discount the amount related in the stock.

## [1] "Ordered  10  Units of product 1:"
## [1] "Ordered  40  Units of product 2:"
## [1] "Ordered  5  Units of product 2:"

Advantages

  • Easy to use.
  • Control the amount you expent on supllies.
  • Ask for the necessary from your suppliers.
  • Control your sales
